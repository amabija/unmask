Who Inspired The Brand?

The brand was inspired by both the love of my life and our precious daughter. Those two have brought an incredible amount of love, light and laughter into my life that it abounds. I had always been a spirited writer but somewhere along the line that part of me got pushed aside.
My purposefully created family has reignited the fervent writer within and I am here to do exploit’s. Motherhood in its own right has exposed parts of me that are both very vulnerable and strangely strong, with very little support from my own family in raising my daughter, I’ve had too singlehandedly learn the in’s and out’s, up’s and downs of what it is to be a virtuous and involved mother, and thank God for placing the most handsome and supportive partner beside me to help me do that, thus it would be criminal to say the brand is mine alone, but it also belongs to those who inspired it.
