The Brand

Hi there Superstar, a very warm welcome to Unmasking Trauma!
Unmasking Trauma is a platform in which I created to regain my voice, and other lost parts of myself.
The thing is I’ve always been a loud personality. Black, bold and beautiful, witty, well-spoken and confident, a high vibrating and genuinely happy soul, but somewhere along the line, the world seemed to extinguish my fire.
My fire has been extinguished through child abandonment, physical and emotional abuse, racial injustices/cultural indifference, dysfunctional family systems, substance abuse, bullying, and so much more!
My blog is my voice, it serves as a poised podium to unpack all things related to the trauma’s I’ve endured, while it is also a pure pathway to the pieces of myself I’ve lost along the way. I formally or informally invite you, (whichever you prefer) on this powerful journey, as I re-discover and re-collect all of who I was and who I am meant to be. I can only hope that through the lenses of words in my journey, you too may identify the broken pieces within yourself, pick them up with grace and begin your own journey to healing, because it can’t just be about me right? Let’s make it about us.
I promise to approach this with full transparency and gentleness to your and - my heart.
Thank you Superstar, for reading, and agreeing to do this with me, it means everything.
